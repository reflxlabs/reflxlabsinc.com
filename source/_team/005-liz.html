Liz Hardy loves storytelling and using stories to help clients and companies connect with their customers.

As Director of Communications at REFLX Labs, she helps spread the word about the company through social media, web, and traditional media outlets.

Liz has over 20 years of experience in the software, web, and mobile industries, working with companies such as AT&T, Nordstrom, REI, T-Mobile, RealNetworks, and more. She also has a successful freelance content strategy business, and from writing and editing, to project management and organizational development, Liz knows how to wear a lot of hats at once.

After living throughout the U.S. and overseas, Liz moved to Seattle for college and has been here ever since. She still has the travel bug and always has a trip to some far-flung destination in the works.

Liz has a B.A. in English and a B.A. in Geography from the University of Washington. She also holds multiple technical certificates from the University of Washington Extension and is certified in project management from the Project Management Institute.
